About Us

Our team consists of four passionate students dedicated to revolutionizing the renewable energy landscape. Through our major project, we harness the power of geospatial analysis to predict the future generation of renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, hydro, and biomass across various Indian states. By integrating meteorological, climatic change, and existing energy data with spatial datasets, we strive to offer valuable insights for informed decision-making towards a sustainable energy future. Leveraging geostatistical modelling and machine learning techniques, we aim to pave the way for a greener and more sustainable tomorrow
